Plague: Conspiracy Fall 2020 Edition

I became a senior editor and web designer for Plague Magazine at NYU. Our fall 2020 issue was completely coded and designed by me.

My concept for our digital issue came from my experience of the internet in the 2000s. Image maps and interactive graphical elements made clicking on every link feel like an immersive, pick-your-own-adventure novel. Through leading a team of writers and artists, we created an interactive digital Plague experience. Most graphic image elements were designed by me with Adobe Photoshop.
