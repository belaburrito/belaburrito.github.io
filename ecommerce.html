eCommerce SPA

This project started as front end developer test for a job interview. I've continued developing it in order to hone my skills with React, React hooks and routing, and Axios.

It is a SPA for an eCommerce site, with a product list page and product details page. The product data is fetched from a sample API. You can also use a search bar in order to filter products by their name.
